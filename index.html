warehouses

Civil engineers design and build warehouses, focusing on structural strength, efficient layouts, and safety. They ensure the building can handle heavy loads, optimize space, and meet safety standards.

schools

Civil engineers design and construct schools, ensuring safety, structural integrity, and functionality. They focus on creating durable buildings with proper classrooms, utilities, and outdoor spaces, meeting safety and accessibility standards.

Road

Civil engineers design and construct roads, focusing on safety, durability, and efficient traffic flow. They plan routes, manage drainage, and ensure proper materials are used to withstand heavy traffic and weather conditions.
